How Does Argon SMS Work?

Argon SMS has been designed to allow you to send mass SMS messages to your group members in a convenient way. Our current customers use Argon SMS to alert their users on: product restocks, exclusive access or important information.

Unlike other SMS providers our monthly service includes the cost of messages where you can send up to 1 message a day to 100 users, that's 3100 SMS messages a month all included in the monthly fee starting at £50 / $65. If you have more users or want to send more messages, that's no problem, we can sort that too!
